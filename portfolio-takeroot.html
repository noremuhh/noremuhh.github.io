Take Root

Take Root is an after-school garden-based science and nutrition outreach program that is offered at Shaw Middle School. The task at hand for our Senior Capstone class was to create a logo, promotional poster, certificate, recognition poster, and a t-shirt design. This was a group collaboration by Bella Chavez, Nikky Hwang, Christina Courtney and myself.

  • Client: Shaw Middle School
  • Date: Fall 2018
