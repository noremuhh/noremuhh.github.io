Killer Lips

Task:

Killer Lips is a mock lipstick brand that empowers women to wear any shade in this line and basically “kill” the show and embrace their lip beauty with this product. Whether that may be going out on a date, running errands, or just a pick me up lipstick. Killer Lips lipstick comes in a variety of 50 different shades and each shade can be purchased as either matte, velvet, or sheer. So in total 150 different varieties of lipstick. Killer Lips derived its name from the slang term “killer” not actually a killer, but killer meaning like excellent, stylish, like knocking them dead with style. But style for your lips.
